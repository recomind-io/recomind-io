# RecoMind

**Personalization intelligence for your store.** Increase conversion and cut CAC by understanding why each shopper buys or walks away.

Most stores do not have a traffic problem — they have an understanding problem. Visitors search, browse, and fill carts, but the store never learns what they want, so only a fraction buy and three of four carts never check out. The tools that could fix it were priced for companies 50x your size. RecoMind unlocks that same personalization engine for your store: one profile of each shopper, powering every surface, live in days.

## 🧠 One brain across every surface

- 🔍 **Personalized Search** — hybrid search that combines keywords, meaning, and this shopper's taste, so ready buyers stop bouncing.
- ✨ **Recommendations** — personal, real-time, inventory-aware suggestions that grow the basket without a discount.
- 💬 **AI Salesperson** — a seller chatbot built on a recommendation engine, not a FAQ bot: it knows the full catalog, speaks in your brand voice, and closes sales.
- 👤 **Customer 360** — one profile per shopper, from champions to at-risk, telling you who to contact and what to sell next.
- 📊 **Market Intelligence** — demand trends and your own unanswered searches, so you stock and feature what shoppers are about to want.

All five surfaces pull from the same understanding of each shopper — including brand-new anonymous visitors, whose intent is read within seconds of arriving. No account, no cookie, no history required.

## ⚡ How it works

1. **Understand every shopper**, even the first-time anonymous ones.
2. **Act everywhere, every week** — search, recommendations, chat, and offers reshape around each shopper's intent.
3. **Win new customers with ads that already know them** — segments built from real purchase intent feed your ad channels, cutting CAC on the same spend.

The result: convert far more of the traffic you already pay for, without buying a single extra click. The alternative is stitching together a search app, a rec widget, a chatbot, a CDP, and an ML team — none of which talk to each other — over a year or more. RecoMind is one connected system, live in days, and the only one that increases conversion and cuts CAC at the same time.
